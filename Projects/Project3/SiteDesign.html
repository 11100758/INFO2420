Color Scheme – Utah Backyard Birds

This is the proposed color palette for the Utah Backyard Birds site. The scheme uses natural, earthy tones (greens and browns) to fit the outdoor, backyard-bird theme, with a warm orange accent for link hover. The light cream background keeps the page calm and readable for the target audience of bird watchers and nature lovers.
